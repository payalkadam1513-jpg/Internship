steps to cook maggi

  1. Boil the water and cook the noodles for 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Pour 2 cups of water into a pot and turn the burner to high.
  3. Once the water bubbles, open packets of Maggi and add the noodles.
  4. Stir and boil the noodles until they're tender.
