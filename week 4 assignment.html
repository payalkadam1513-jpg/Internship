Yashoda Technical Campus

Yashoda Technical Campus (YTC) is a technical institute
established in 2011 by the Yashoda Shikshan Prasarak Mandal
(YSPM) in Satara, Maharashtra. It offers various undergraduate
and postgraduate courses in engineering and technology, approved by
AICTE, New Delhi, and the Government of Maharashtra.

History

Yashoda Technical Campus, located in Wadhe, Satara, was
established in 2011 by the Yashoda Shikshan Prasarak Mandal (YSPM).
The institution was founded with the vision of providing quality
technical and management education, fostering a professional
environment for students.

Vision

Yashoda Technical Campus aims to be a leader in technical and
management education, focusing on providing value-based, quality
education with world-class infrastructure and strong industry linkages.

Mission

Yashoda Technical Campus's mission is to provide quality education
that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and a positive attitude
to meet future challenges and contribute to industry and society.
