1. How can I reset my password?

    To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your email.

    • Check your spam folder if the email doesn't arrive.
    • Use a secure password manager for stronger passwords.

  2. Why can't I log in to my account?

    There could be multiple reasons why you're unable to log in. Refer to the table below for common issues and solutions.

    Issue Solution
    Incorrect Password Use the password reset option to create a new password.
    Account Locked Wait 30 minutes or contact support to unlock your account.
    Email Not Verified Check your inbox for the verification email and follow the link.

  3. How do I update my billing information?

    To update billing details, go to your account settings, select "Billing," and update your credit card or PayPal information.

    • Ensure your billing address matches the one linked to your payment method.
    • Changes may take a few minutes to reflect in your account.
