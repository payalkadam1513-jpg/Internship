Description About Restaurant

Hotel Chandravilas is a well-known establishment in Satara, with two main branches: Chandravilas Resto and Chandravilas Bhuvan.
Chandravilas Resto, located in Raviwar Peth, is a multi-cuisine restaurant offering a variety of Indian, Chinese, Other dishes,
while Chandravilas Bhuvan, in Malhar Peth, is known for its Maharashtrian, Gujarati,
South Indian sweets and snacks. speciality of this resto is hand-pounded masalas, Indian, Chinese, Thai, Mexican, Italian,
American dishes, including Palak Patta Chaat, Paneer Rawalpindi, and Sizzlers.
